COVID-19 Precautions

Please do not come to the Clinic if you have been in contact with anyone suspected with COVID or have any of the following symptoms: fever, cough, sore throat, running nose & shortness of breath. Masks are mandatory. All bookings on the website are telephone appointment only.


Masking Requirements

Please note that masks are still required for all hospital visits at this time. Kindly keep yourself and our staff safe by putting on your mask before entering the clinic.
